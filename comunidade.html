What is PM2.5?

Fine particles smaller than 2.5 micrometers that penetrate deeply into the lungs.

Health Effects

Can cause respiratory and cardiovascular issues, and worsen asthma.

How to Protect Yourself

Use an N95 mask, avoid outdoor activities on bad days, keep windows closed.
